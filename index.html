About This Site

In consideration of the development of electronic media, this website aims to explore the future society brought about by information and communication technology. Additionally, it seeks to design a human-centered society for the well-being of people through data collaboration and utilization to address societal challenges.

This site serves as a research hub and information sharing platform by the members of Endo Lab. We focus on studying the impact of information technologies on society, and our research materials contribute to the collective understanding of creating a more livable and human-centric community.

More information about our lab can be found on our MDG lab's official website.
